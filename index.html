About Us

Overview

Established in 2017, WAHT ALMSTQBL ALRYDT, an ISOCertified SME Company specializes in Engineering Consultancy, EPC (Engineering, Procurement, Construction) projects, as well as electrical, mechanical, and civil work, pipeline and substation constructions. We handle the design, procurement, and construction of large-scale projects in various industries, with special focus on energy industry. Moreover, we provide end-to-end solutions, from initial planning to project completion, ensuring efficient and cost-effective project delivery. Dedicated to exceeding client expectations, we aspire to be an innovative, trusted Contractor committed to strong customer satisfaction.We pride ourselves on innovation, reliability, and nurturing local partnerships to enhance in-country value.

Harweel Cluster Phase 2 Facilities (EPC)

Project Overview

This project focuses on the construction and commissioning of comprehensive oil and gas processing facilities to enhance capacity and operational efficiency.

Project Benefits

  • Enhanced oil and gas processing capacities
  • Increased operational efficiency and reliability
  • Sustainable and safe practices

Scope of Work

  • Oil/Gas Separators: Three-stage trains with a 45,000 BPD capacity
  • Booster Compressors: Three-stage booster compressor trains
  • Condensate Stabilizer Unit
  • Gas Sweetening and Dehydration Units:
    • Sulfinol process unit (75 MSCFPD)
    • TEG process units (export: 75 MSCFPD, injection: 200 MSCFPD)
  • Injection Compressor: 200 MSCFPD at 600 bar
  • Gas Conditioning Unit: 75 MSCFPD capacity
  • Export Oil Storage and Pumping:
    • Two storage tanks (63,000 barrels each)
    • Pump with 11,000 cubic meters per day capacity
  • Pipeline and Trunk Line Construction
  • Manifold Stations and Well Hook-ups
  • Electrical and Substation Works:
    • 132 kV and 33 kV Overhead Lines (OHL)/Substation
    • Comprehensive site preparation, fencing, and trenching
    • Four RCC buildings, structural steel, equipment installation, extensive piping, and flowlines

Yibal Khuff Off-Plot Project – PDO

One of the largest projects in PDO’s recent history, the Yibal Khuff project involved constructing 47 well sites, pipelines, a Gas Turbine Generator power plant, substations, overhead power lines, control rooms, and security systems.

Introduction

Spanning an area of 1.68km², the Yibal Khuff Project (YKP) is the secondlargest and most technically complex project in PDO's history. Located approximately 350 kilometers southwest of Muscat, the facility can deliver 6.1 MSCMD of gas, around 21,900 BPD of oil, and 235 TPD of Sulphur. YKP holds high strategic value in meeting Oman’s growing medium and long-term oil and gas demands and reducing PDO's net nonassociated gas import.

Scope of Work

  • Design & Engineering:
    • Flow lines, pipelines, and well hook-up designs
  • Procurement:
    • High-quality materials and equipment procurement
  • Construction:
    • Flow lines, pipelines, well hook-ups, and overhead lines
    • Installation of beam pumps and associated electrical/instrumentation works
  • Comprehensive testing and commissioning

Project Benefits

  • Enhanced infrastructure and operational capacity
  • Streamlined operations and increased efficiency
  • Sustainability and safety-focused work environment
